Our Story

Authentic Tanzania,
Masterfully Crafted

Founded in Arusha in 2025 by a former safari guide, WildRush Safaris was born from a deep love for Tanzania's wild places and a desire to share them honestly. We are locally owned, locally driven — and fiercely proud of it.

From the endless plains of the Serengeti to the icy summit of Kilimanjaro and the turquoise shores of Zanzibar, we craft journeys that go beyond the ordinary — personalised itineraries, expert naturalist guides, and a commitment to responsible travel that protects the land we love.

Game Drives

Kilimanjaro Climbs

Guided ascents via Lemosho, Machame, and Marangu routes with full support crew and altitude acclimatization protocols.

Balloon Safaris

Float silently over the Serengeti at sunrise — the most breathtaking vantage point in all of Africa.

🌊

Zanzibar Escapes

Dive the coral reefs, sail a dhow at sunset, and lose yourself in the spice-scented streets of Stone Town.

🦒

Walking Safaris

Experience the bush at ground level — tracking footprints, reading the landscape, feeling the rawness of wild Africa.

🎻

Cultural Tours

Visit Maasai villages, meet the Hadzabe bushmen, and share a meal with a Chagga family on the slopes of Kilimanjaro.

🦅

Birdwatching

Tanzania's 1,000+ bird species — from flamingos on Lake Manyara to the rare shoebill in the remote western parks.

📸

Photo Safaris

Specialist photography vehicles, extended golden-hour stops, and expert guidance for capturing Africa's wildest moments.
